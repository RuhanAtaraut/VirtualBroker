The Existing System of finding the rooms,PGs or houses for rent will takes place manually. It means that the people who are in need of room/houses, they have to find by visiting each and every places of a particular area where they want to stay.

  • Problem 1: Waste of Time And Energy. Or if that is not possible for them, then they wish to take the help of Brokers based on Commission. Broker will charge to show the rooms/houses. This Commission is not only from customers side but also from the owner who belongs to that asset.

  • Problem 2: Cost Efficiency.(costly) Therefore this existing System is not reliable for new comers or the one who is new for the place or a city.

Proposed system:

The Proposed System come-up with the solution for the problem faced earlier. Proposed system acts as a bridge between the customer (user) and the owner of the rooms/houses. This system mainly has two parts. One is with owner’s point of view and on the other hand user’s point of view.
If owner wants to upload the details of his belonging information about rooms/houses like, Name, City, Area, Complete Address, Contact details and the price. All the details which he/she has entered will get stored in the Database.

Similarly for the user/customer system provides two filters. .

Those are:
i. Filter by price
ii. Filter by city

By choosing the filters and submitting, user/customer will get all those details of rooms/houses which are available in the database with respect to the chosen filters. .

• Advantages of proposed system:

 Easy to use i.e. User friendly.
 Cost effective.
 Reliable.
 Saves lots of time and energy.
